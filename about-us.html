About Us

The objective of our business is to offer all customers cost-effective, friendly service with clean bins. We are dedicated to provide all customers in Auckland with a high level of service, and we have been providing a quality bin hiring service for more than 12 years.

From the smallest 2m² skip to the largest 9m² are available. When you input your suburb on our website, you can see all of the dumpsters. By comparing the man in front of each bin, you may get a decent sense of the size needed for your bin rental.

When you hire a bin from us you can expect a professional service from a bin company with over twelve years of operating in and around Auckland.
